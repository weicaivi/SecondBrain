# Knowledge Base

Evergreen notes — ideas that have been processed, connected, and are worth keeping long-term.

```
03-Knowledge/
├── Concepts/     Mental models, frameworks, recurring ideas
├── People/       Notes on thinkers/creators — their POV, your evolving take
└── Tech/         Technical concepts, patterns, architecture decisions
```

**What belongs here:**
- Insights that survived at least one night's sleep
- Ideas that connect to 2+ other things you know
- Frameworks you actually use in thinking/work

**What does NOT belong here:**
- Raw source notes (those go in `04-Sources/`)
- Daily ephemera (stays in `01-Daily/`)

**Naming convention:** Use plain descriptive titles, not dates.
- Good: `The Bitter Lesson.md`, `Calibration vs Accuracy.md`
- Bad: `2026-03-17 karpathy note.md`
